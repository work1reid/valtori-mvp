# 🎯 Valtori AI - Sales Practice Simulator

**Practice sales calls without burning leads. Get scored like a trader. Improve with data.**

Valtori AI is a voice-enabled sales training platform that lets sales reps practice real conversations with AI customers, receive detailed performance analytics, and track improvement over time — all without risking actual leads.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 🎤 **Real Voice Simulation**
- Speak naturally into your microphone
- AI customers respond with realistic objections
- Different AI personalities (skeptical, friendly, busy)
- Can hang up on poor pitches or say "yes" to great ones

### 📊 **FXReplay-Style Analytics**
- Close rate tracking (sales / total calls)
- Commission earnings tracker
- Performance trends (weekly/monthly)
- Personal best scores
- Practice history with full transcripts

### 🎮 **Gamified Training**
- Virtual sales and commission tracking
- Score: 0-100 on every call
- 4 key metrics:
  - Closing Ability
  - Objection Handling
  - Confidence & Energy
  - Rapport Building

### 🤖 **Adaptive AI**
- Gets harder as you improve
- Tailored to your average score
- Industry-specific scenarios
- Real objections and pushback

## 📖 How to Use

1. **Click "Get Started"**
2. **Choose Your Industry** from 10 B2B options
3. **Start Your Call** - AI greets you
4. **Click Mic 🎤** to speak
5. **Get Scored** - Detailed performance breakdown

### Possible Outcomes
- ✅ **Sale Closed** - Cha-ching! Commission earned
- ❌ **Hang Up** - AI ends call if not impressed
- 📞 **Manual End** - You control when to stop
